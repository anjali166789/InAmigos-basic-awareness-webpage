About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social impact through education, food distribution, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and youth development.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Providing food, clothing and support to underprivileged communities.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Empowering children through quality education and learning opportunities.

Project JEEV

Supporting animal welfare through feeding and rescue initiatives.

Project UDAAN

Helping women achieve financial independence through skill development.

Project PRAKRITI

Promoting environmental sustainability through plantation drives.

Project VIKAS

Providing internship and skill-building opportunities for youth.
